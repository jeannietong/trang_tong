EGG TART

  • by: Trang Tong

  • date: May 12, 2021

Egg tart with crispy, golden crust is extremely beautiful, blended with greasy cake filling is a favorite of many people. So am I. When I was in Vietnam, I always ordered tart from the bakery near my house and was wondering why they could make a perfect flavour like that or if they have any secret recipe/tips to bake. During the winter break, I started learning how to make it and tried many recipes from other food bloggers. I succeed in baking tart at the first time. Good smell, sweet taste and crunchy cake base are those things I can comment on my first try.

I have made egg tart for the fifth time so I think I got some tips to share with you.

If you bake the cake yourself, you will see the happiness in watching the cake turns out in the oven and the moment take it out with its delicious aroma ^^.

Everyone should try it and give me suggestions on how to make it perfectly as well as creative versions of the recipe!

With this recipe, I made 7-10 tarts. Timing: 1 hour

MAKING CRUST:

- Add unsalted butter to flour and mix by hand. Dissolve salt and sugar in water then pour into the flour mixture and mix to knead.

- Use a roller to flatten the dough and spread the butter evenly on it.

- Fold the dough into a square shape, wrap it up and chill in the fridge for an hour.

- After the required time, take it out and flatten the dough again. Spread the butter on it and follow up the procedure like the first time, repeat for 3 times.

- Gently beat the egg yolk and add sugar then mix well.

- Add corn starch, mix well and set aside for 5 minutes.

- Continue to add pasteurized milk, heavy cream and condensed milk. In order to have the best golden smooth filling surface, gently mix until the mixture is smooth, filter through a sieve to catch any lumps.

- Chill in the fridge for 30 minutes.

- Put the butter on the mold. Cut the chilled dough into small pieces and place into a mold for each.

- Press the center of a piece of dough firmly then fill the custard.

NOTE: Pour the custard about 90% of the mold because it is still rising. Otherwise, it will overflow and not look very nice.

- Bake at 180¬∞C for 30-35 minutes until golden.

- Depend on the oven, continue to bake for 5-10 minutes until those cakes have a good golden color.

- Remove them from the oven and place on a rack to cool (if wanted). They sizzle when you first take them out. Freshly egg art will be very fragrant, crispy and greasy.
