I love art. I attended many sketching courses in college to improve my art skill although it's not my major. Fine-art has a creative beauty that comes to artists' mind and they draw it on paper or canvas, etc like the way showing the admiration for the majestic beauty of nature, human, fashion or even little things around us.

For me, art is a language of its own, I love to create something without words, something that speaks to people.
